    Spaghe'tti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs,cheese, pancetta, and black pepper. It's creamy, rich, and incredibly flavorful.

    • 200g spaghetti
    • 100g pancetta or guanciale
    • 2 large eggs
    • 50g Pecorino Romano cheese
    • 50g Parmesan cheese
    • Black pepper
    • Salt

    Instructions:

    1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente.
    2. Fry the pancetta or guanciale until crispy.
    3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, Pecorino Romano cheese,and parmesan cheese.
    4. Drain the cooked spaghetti and add it to the pancetta. Toss to combine.
    5. Remove from heat and quickly mix in the egg and cheese mixture.
    6. Season with black pepper and serve immediately.

    Classic Beef Lasagna

    Description: A delicious and hearty Italian dish made with layers of pasta, seasoned beef, ricotta cheese, and marinara sauce.

    Ingredients:

    • 1 pound ground beef
    • 1 onion, chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 (15-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
    • 2 cups ricotta cheese
    • 12 lasagna noodles, cooked and drained

    Cooking Instructions:

    1. In a skillet, cook beef, onion, and garlic until browned; drain.
    2. Stir in crushed tomatoes and simmer for 30 minutes.
    3. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta cheese with an egg.
    4. Spread a layer of meat sauce in a baking dish, followed by noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers.
    5. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 30 minutes.

    Delicioucs Margherita Pizza

    Description: Enjoy a classic Margherita pizza with fresh tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, and basil leaves.

    Ingredients: Dough, tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, basil leaves, olive oil.

    Cooking Instructions:

    1. Preheat the oven.
    2. preheat your oven to 450f (230C) and place a pizza stone or baking sheet inside to heat up.
    3. prepare the Dough.
    4. Roll out your pizza dough on floured surface to your desired thickness. place it on parchment paper for easy transfer.
    5. Add sauce and Toppings.
    6. spread pizza sauce evenly oven the dough and add your favorite toppings such as cheese,vegetables,and meats.
    7. Bake the pizza
    8. Silde the parchment paper with the pizza onto the preheated pizza stone or baking sheet. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
    9. Serve and Enjoy
